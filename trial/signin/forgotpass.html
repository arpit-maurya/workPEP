Password recovery

Follow the instructions:

1.Fill in your email address (which is the login to your BedBooking account ), and then click the 'SEND'.

2. Sign in to your email account and open the message from BedBooking*.

3. Follow the instructions in the email received from BedBooking.

4. Go to Login and then log in using your new password.

* If you do not see the email from BedBooking, check the folder SPAM in your email inbox. The message may be classified by your email software as SPAM. In case of problems, please contact us.
